It starts when we pull the platform that holds the wheel up, then this happens:

  1. The wheel goes down the ramp and hits a lever
  2. The lever tilts the top and a marble falls down also on a ramp
  3. The marble goes on a track and goes downhill once again
  4. The marble goes into a funnel
  5. The marble gets directed by the funnel to hit a key on a computer, and the process is complete

That is how our machine works and what it accomplishes, thanks for reading!!!
